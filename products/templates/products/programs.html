fitness-xpress

Fitness-Xpress is suitable if: you don't work out, exercise just a little or have difficulty getting started! You exercise for 45 minutes a day for 30 days and follow a 4 step workout program.

Steps 1-3 start with your upper body: cardio, leg, core for 3 x 13 min = 39 minutes. Step 4 is high intensity: Upper body, legs, cardio for 6 minutes. You will get new exercises every day and in total you get 92 different exercises and challenges, where there are 36 different strength and cardio exercise, 26 high intensity exercises and 30 challenges.

Mamma-Shape

Mamma-Shape is especially designed for you who have given birth to children over the past three years.

The exercises are simple to perform and very effective. It is a mixture of strength exercises and short cardio workouts. The first few days we start quietly so you can regain control of your muscles. We then gradually increase the intensity so that you will get the desired result. We exchange an exercise every day so it never gets boring. In total, you get 35 different exercises.
